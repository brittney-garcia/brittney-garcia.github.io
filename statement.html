Artist Statement

Who are you and what do you care about as an artist? What kind of subjects do you explore in your work?

On the surface, I am a full-time student that is currently trying to get a BFA in Graphic Design. I like dogs, and long walks on the beach. And pina coladas and getting caught in the rain. But as an artist, I desire my work to reflect the inner desires, feelings, and depths of human emotion but portayed in abstract forms. I have also dabbled in the media of film, animation, and media art.

How does the approaches you take in your work reflect your interests?

Much of what I have done so far has been abstract. I try to show these ideas pieces of myself in hopes that people who see it can relive that same experience. I don't always inherently choose my subjects or topics, but rather I try to create a story or images from a piece or emotion and build a story from there.
