M@SS M3D1A, Net Art, 2018

M@SS M3D1A

In an ever-growing digital world, it is easy to fall under the massive waves of media and influence. We are naturally curious and often can't help our feelings of indulgence with the glorified promise of immediate gratification. "Mass Media" is a commentary on our innate need to satisfy this feeling.
